Tree Pruning Prioritization Solution

Solution Overview

Our solution provides a comprehensive approach to tree pruning prioritization and vegetation management. By leveraging a multi-criteria approach, it optimizes maintenance decisions for electrical networks and other areas requiring environmental upkeep, such as highways, railways, urban green spaces, telecommunications lines, industrial sites, airports, and water reservoir areas.

Technical Description

The solution integrates seamlessly with IBM EIS – Vegetation Management to enhance and refine vegetation management plans. It imports data from shapefiles provided by EIS Vegetation, processes it to include additional criteria such as social and biotechnical factors, and generates optimized pruning plans. This process ensures that maintenance is both efficient and effective.

  • Shapefile Importer: Imports data from shapefiles provided by IBM EIS – Vegetation Management. This block receives the shapefile data and prepares it for processing.
  • Multi-criteria optmizer: Applies a multi-criteria approach to the imported data, considering social, environmental, and biotechnical factors. This block refines the data to create actionable pruning plans.
  • Plan Generator: Uses the refined data to generate detailed pruning plans and recommendations for maintenance tasks.
  • Outputs and Implementation: Delivers the final pruning plans and reports. These outputs are made accessible via the SaaS platform for implementation and ongoing management.
